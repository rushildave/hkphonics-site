Meet the Founder

My journey began with managing a EuroKids Preschool, where one question inspired me every day:
"How can children become confident readers instead of simply memorizing words?"

This curiosity led me to explore different phonics approaches and understand how children actually learn to read. During the COVID-19 pandemic, while teaching phonics online, I realized that many Indian children, who learn English as a second or third language, benefit from a teaching approach tailored to their learning needs.

Drawing on years of classroom experience, research, and continuous refinement, I developed HK Phonics—a structured early literacy framework designed for Indian learners. It combines systematic phonics, reading, spelling, and practical teaching strategies to help children become confident, independent readers.

My mission is simple:

To empower teachers and inspire every child to read with confidence.

– Hetal Kulkarni
Founder, HK Phonics
